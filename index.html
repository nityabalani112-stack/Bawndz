How do I join the BAWNDZ network?

Simply register with your institutional email, complete your professional profile, and start connecting with alumni. Our guided onboarding process will help you maximize your networking potential.

Is my personal and professional data secure?

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption, GDPR compliance, and advanced privacy controls to protect all alumni and institutional data.

How does the smart matching system work?

Our AI analyzes your professional background, career goals, and interests to match you with relevant alumni mentors, career opportunities, and networking events. The system continuously learns to provide better matches.
